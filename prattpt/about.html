About US

Our innovative facility focuses on the restoration of mobility and function combined with maintenance protocols for an overall healthier approach to life.

M. Ashton Pratt, DPT

Dr. M. Ashton Pratt has a Doctorate of Physical Therapy from the Medical University of South Carolina with additional training in specialty areas including Vestibular, Post Mastectomy Lymphedema, Fibrotic and Scar tissue manipulation. She has a significant interest and clinical training in Sports Injury Rehabilitation with emphasis in manual based rehabilitation including state of the art Vibrastim. Dr. Pratt understands and is acutely aware of lower body biomechanics & the rehabilitation process secondary to being a collegiate athlete at University of South Carolina.

Maureen Bernard, PTA

Maureen has an extensive background in physical therapy with 14 years of experience including the Naval Base Clinic. Maureen is diligent, demands excellence, and ensures that each and every patient receives the best quality of care during their rehab. In her free time, Maureen enjoys kayaking and exploring Pinterest for new home projects!

Rachel Cunningham, ATC

Rachel attended the Athletic Training Program at University of South Carolina graduating in 2014. She is currently pursuing further education in rehabilitation to obtain her PTA degree. Rachel enjoys basketball and collegiate sports, especially updating Dr. Pratt on ESPN highlights.
